Team Aurora Borealis competed in the #Challenge4ClimateAction Datathon Stream hosted by Convergence.Tech in March/April of 2021. We placed second among over 70 teams from across the world. We explored the correlation between carbon pricing policies, and carbon emissions. We provided a list of 20 Calls to Action for various stakeholders.
